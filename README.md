# 9. MAINTENANCE STRUCTURE (CLEAR MODEL)

### Maintenance Wallet

- Each landlord maintains a **Maintenance Wallet**  
- Topped up yearly  
- Used for landlord-responsibility repairs  
- Full reports provided  

---

### A. Tenant-Caused Maintenance ‚Äì Tenant Pays

- Broken doors  
- Misuse damage  
- Blocked WC from misuse  
- Damaged fittings  

Landlord NOT contacted.

---

### B. General/Wear & Tear ‚Äì Landlord via Wallet

- Plumbing  
- Electrical aging  
- Pump servicing  
- Painting after exit  

Handled from wallet without disturbing landlord.

---

### C. Emergency Maintenance ‚Äì Landlord Contacted

- Major leak  
- Fire risk  
- Structural danger  

DomiHive acts first ‚Üí reports ‚Üí funds used.

### D. General Shared Maintenance

Where applicable, tenants contribute transparently while DomiHive provides vetted vendors and supervision.
